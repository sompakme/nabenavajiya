How To Buy Amazon Gift Card With Venmo

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Monday, August 12, 2024
People are currently reading this guide.

So You Want to Shower Someone (or Yourself) with Amazon Goodness, But Your Wallet Feels a Little Light? Enter Venmo, Stage Left!

Let's face it, Amazon is basically our digital playground these days. From the perfect birthday present for your niece (who seems to have everything) to that ridiculously comfy pair of llama-patterned slippers you just had to have (don't judge!), it's a one-stop shop for, well, everything. But what happens when your bank account is channeling its inner Scrooge McDuck and refusing to budge? Fear not, fellow shopper extraordinaire, for there's a knight in slightly-tarnished armor riding in on a virtual pony – Venmo!

Venmo to the Rescue: How to Buy an Amazon Gift Card with the App You Already Use to Split Dinner Bills with Friends (and Maybe That One Time You Owed Your Roommate for Toilet Paper)

Yes, you read that right. Venmo, the app that's seen its fair share of emoji-laden transactions for brunch and questionable late-night purchases, can now be your gateway to Amazon glory. Here's how this digital magic trick works:

  • Open the Venmo app and prepare to unleash your inner gift-giving guru. Just picture the look on your friend's face when they realize that Amazon package hurtling towards their doorstep is thanks to you (and your mad Venmo skills).

  • Tap the ever-so-slightly-confusing "Pay/Request" button. We know, it throws us off every time too. But trust us, Venmo wisdom lies on the other side.

  • Channel your inner detective and search for the lucky recipient. Be it your bestie who needs a retail therapy session or your perpetually indecisive co-worker for a Secret Santa gift, type in their name and get ready to shower them with virtual currency (that can be redeemed for actual stuff – mind blown!).

  • Here comes the hero of the story: The Gift Icon! Don't worry, it won't be wearing a cape, but it will be responsible for your next Amazon shopping spree (sort of). Tap that little guy and prepare to be amazed.

  • Welcome to the land of gift card options! Venmo might not have a llama-patterned slipper selection (yet), but they do have a decent spread of Amazon gift card denominations. Pick your poison (or, you know, the perfect amount) and get ready to hit that glorious "Purchase" button.

    • Bonus Tip: Venmo likes to keep things interesting, so there might be a fee associated with your gift card purchase. But hey, compared to the joy of gifting (or the thrill of treating yourself), it's a small price to pay!

    And There You Have It! You've Officially Conquered the Art of Gifting with Venmo

    So there you have it, folks! Now you can be the hero of every gifting occasion, the master of online shopping sprees (funded by Venmo, of course), and the envy of all your friends who are still stuck writing out actual checks for birthday gifts. Now, if you'll excuse me, I have a very important date with a pair of llama-patterned slippers and a one-click checkout button. Happy gifting!
