FAQs

Can I get free shipping from Everlane?

Yes. Everlane has free shipping for any US order over $100. There are also standard and expedited shipping options available, for an additional cost. Our page will sometimes have Everlane free shipping promo codes to help you save on your order.  

When does Everlane have sales?

Everlane has a sale section that is always on its website and updated each season. You can shop up to 70% off selected clothing with their sale. They also have seasonal sales such as Black Friday and Cyber Monday. If you sign up for their newsletter, you can also shop for exclusive sales too. 

Does Everlane have a welcome offer?

Yes, when you sign up to receive the official newsletter, you will also receive an Everland 15% off code to use on your first order. On top of that, you will be the first to hear about their newest products, latest sales, and receive updates on their hottest collections. 

Does Everlane offer promo codes?

Everlane promo codes are usually available, including free shipping, percentage discounts, and an Everlane first order promo code. You'll be able to get your jeans, tote bags, or shoes for less when you use one of our Everlane promo codes. Simply click on the relevant promo code and add this to your order before you pay. We'd recommend double-checking the terms and conditions of your Everlane promo code to check your order is eligible.

What is the Everlane returns policy?

The Everlane returns policy states that it is happy to accept returns made within 30 days of the shipping date, providing that items are clearly unworn and unused. Unfortunately, shipping costs are non-refundable, so customers will have to cover these themselves. 

Hints and Tips

Exclusive Offers: Sign up to receive Everlane's text message alerts and get exclusive offers, early access to sales events, and updates on new collections. Signing up is simple, and you can opt out whenever you like. It’s a great way to make sure that you never miss an opportunity to save at Everlane. 

Sale Section: Shop the year-round sale section at Everlane and find hundreds of styles at discounted prices. This section includes clothing, swimwear, underwear, shoes, and accessories at steep discounts of up to 70%. Sale items can often not be returned or exchanged, so make sure you don’t miss out on making some excellent savings. 

Refer a Friend: Create a free account at Everlane and refer friends to receive credit toward your next purchase. You’ll receive a unique referral link that you can send to others. Once your friends create their own accounts, they can use your link to shop, and you will receive a $25 credit toward your next purchase once your order ships.

Seasonal Sales: Discover the seasonal sales where you can make some big savings on dresses, denim, accessories, and more. Seasonal sale dates at Everlane have included Cyber Monday, Black Friday, and Christmas. Make sure to check their website at these seasonal times for some of the best savings on your orders.
