I Spend 8 Hours a Day on Amazon, and Im Buying These 8 Spring Picks

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Sunday, August 4, 2024

As a shopping expert and avid planner, I’m already filling my cart with all things spring. I scour Amazon like it’s my job because it actually is, and I sifted through pages and pages of products to pluck out the eight finds I can’t resist snagging. Below, browse through a few of my existing favorites, along with a few fresh items I’m adding to my fashion and beauty arsenals ahead of next season. Oh, and I did I mention prices start at just $8?  

The following curation includes transitional wardrobe staples and chic accessories, plus makeup bag must-haves that’ll make you wonder how you lived without them. Shop hidden gems including Reebok sneakers, Sunday Riley skincare, Color Wow hair-care, and a trendy purse from celeb-loved brand, JW Pei. Just be sure to grab your favorite editor-approved finds while they’re still on sale for up to 47 percent off.  

Best Editor-Loved Fashion and Beauty Items 

Reebok Club C 85 Vintage Sneakers

Stylish white sneakers are a timeless essential in every year-round outfit rotation, and now that spring is nearly here, they’re a non-negotiable. Luckily, the quintessential Reebok Club C 85 Vintage Sneakers, are on sale. The now-$73 kicks have a classic silhouette that can be paired with practically anything in your closet; wear them with jeans, dresses, or joggers — the options are endless. The signature style is comfy, too, since it has a cushioned midsole and breathable upper that’s fit for all-day wear. One reviewer confirmed, saying they wore the sneakers to walk “over 20,000 steps” and the pair kept them “comfortable and stylish all the way.” 

Lillusory Collared Cardigan Sweater

Another fashion find worthy of a spot in your shopping cart, this Lillusory cardigan is now on sale for $30. As I prepare to navigate the awkward sartorial period between winter and spring, I’m certain this sweater will become my new best friend. It’s made from a cotton blend so it’s lightweight for layering, but cozy enough to style solo, too. The transitional basic has an elevated feel thanks to its chic collar, ribbed material, and slightly oversized fit. Personally, I can’t wait to wear mine buttoned-up with jeans, and a shopper confirmed it looks “fantastic over a tank, shirt or a blouse, [too].” If you’re still on the fence, take it from a second customer who described the cardigan as “great quality,” adding that they “bought it in three colors.”      

Color Wow Dream Coat Supernatural Spray

Spring means warmer weather (at last!) and frizzier hair (less exciting). In an effort to get a head start on tackling stray strands, I’m shopping the Color Wow Dream Coat Supernatural Spray. The hair-care product leaves your locks smooth and glossy and blocks humidity through up to four shampoo sessions. According to rave reviews, shoppers adore the secret weapon for sleekness — it’s Amazon’s number one best-selling hair spray, and more than 30,000 people purchased it in the past month alone, per the retailer. Shoppers and I aren’t the only fans of the formula; founder of the brand and celebrity hair stylist, Chris Appleton, uses the product to achieve Jennifer Lopez’s iconic blowouts.
