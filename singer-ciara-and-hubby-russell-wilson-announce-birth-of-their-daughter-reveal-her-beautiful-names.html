Singer Ciara and Hubby Russell Wilson Announce Birth of Their Daughter, Reveal Her Beautiful Names

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Tuesday, October 1, 2024
  • Ciara and her husband Russell Wilson's family is growing, as the singer announced in August that she was heavily pregnant with baby number four
  • The mother of four shared the exciting news via her Instagram account, noting that she was proud of their new bundle of joy
  • The two proud parents have named their young one Amora Princess Wilson, tucked in a warm, cosy bed

American singer Ciara and her husband, Russell Wilson, have announced the birth of their daughter.

How did Ciara announce pregnancy?

In August, the 37-year-old singer joyfully disclosed her pregnancy, anticipating her fourth child and third with her hubby Russell.

The couple chose to share their exhilarating baby news through an Instagram video announcement, which, according to PEOPLE, was artfully filmed by the NFL star during their anniversary escapade to Japan in July.
