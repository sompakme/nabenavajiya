What happens if Im late on my spectrum bill?

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Monday, January 1, 0001

Answer

There is a $8.95 late payment charge for each missed payment.

Spectrum, like the majority of cable providers, charges a late payment fee, and this is no different. It does, however, provide an unusually lengthy grace period: you have thirty days from the day of your bill’s due date to pay it. After that time period has expired, you will be charged a $8.95 late fee.
