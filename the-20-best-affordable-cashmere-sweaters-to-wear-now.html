The 20 Best Affordable Cashmere Sweaters to Wear Now

Posted by Martina Birk on Saturday, August 3, 2024

Club Monaco Boiled Collar Cashmere Sweater

Credit: Amazon

Soft, clean, cozy, and shipping now on Amazon Prime. You can't ask for more from a cashmere sweater.

Uniqlo Cashmere Turtleneck Sweater

Credit: Courtesy

You already knew that Uniqlo is home to the best white t-shirt. (Lisa Aiken, fashion director at Neiman Marcus, agrees.) You may have missed its soft yet pill-free cashmere among all the other basics. Don't overlook this essential again. 

Summersalt The Luxe Cashmere Blend Funnel Neck Sweater

Credit: Summersalt

Start your cashmere collection with a silky, minimal turtleneck that hits for less than $100. Summersalt's sweater pairs as easily with a leather suit set as denim, and five-star reviewers say its quality is unmatched at this price. "I’m really picky about material and this cashmere blend is so soft that I don’t need to wear a tank underneath," one reviewer writes.

Naadam The Essential $75 Cashmere Sweater

Credit: Naadam

An authentic cashmere crewneck for $75 sounds too good to be shoppable. Yet Naadam has found a way to create an accessible cashmere so soft, it's earned more than 50,000 five-star reviews. (As the owner of four $75 sweaters, this editor can agree Naadam's cashmere is good.) It's available in 10+ colors and extended sizes, so anyone can get their cashmere fix. 

Tory Burch Lurex Cashmere Sweater

Credit: Tory Burch

Just in time for the holidays, Tory Burch dressed up a classic cashmere crewneck with a hint of sparkle.

Cuyana Recycled Cashmere Soft Wrap Sweater

Credit: Cuyana

You'll want to get wrapped up in Cuyana's recycled cashmere immediately. Somehow, Cuyana knits get softer with each wear.

Everlane Oversized Stroopwafel Turtleneck

Now 38% Off

Credit: Everlane

This editor can confirm Everlane's cashmere comes out of the box snug and soft—and stays that way through weekly winter use. A Kelly green waffle knit in a boxy, oversize fit is exactly what to wear over a matching set for peak coziness.

Reformation Brody Cashmere Tank and Cardi Set

Tiny cropped sweaters are taking over, cashmere included. Wear this set together with jeans or take a note from street style and pair an A-line skirt with just the cardigan.

J.Crew Cashmere Cable-Knit Relaxed V-Neck Sweater

J.Crew is carrying cashmere in every possible cut up to size 3X: crewneck, cardigan, turtleneck, and more. Of the many, many under-$200 options, our eye went to this preppy cable knit.
