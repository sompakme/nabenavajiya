Im an Amazon Shopping Editor, and This Is What Im Buying for Fall

Posted by Martina Birk on Monday, September 30, 2024

After months of sweaty subways, sticky clothes, and humid weather, I am ready to greet fall with open arms. Bring on the chunky sweaters, fuzzy slippers, cozy blankets, and most importantly, crisp, cool air. Of course, a new season equals a shopping opportunity in my book, and I’ve already got my eye on a few things at Amazon. 

My fall wish list spans the fashion, beauty, and home departments, with prices starting at $11.  From Laneige skincare and Ugg boots to Brooklinen towels and a Paddywax candle, my Amazon cart is stocked with brand-name goodies, and I’m sharing my top 10 picks, below. 

Shop Editor-Approved Amazon Fall Picks:

  • Amazon Essentials Cotton Modal Pajama Set, $29 (Originally $30)
  • Laneige Water Sleeping Face Mask, $32
  • Dazzle Dry Nail Mini Lacquer, $11
  • Ugg Classic Ultra Mini Fashion Boot, $150
  • Paddywax Cypress and Fir Hand-Poured Scented Candle, $23
  • Bedsure Fluffy Faux Fur Throw Blanket, $23 (Originally $25)
  • Spanx Faux Leather Leggings, $98
  • Amazon Essentials Cable Knit Sweater, $24 (Originally $35)
  • Lillusory Long-Sleeve Sweater and Pants Lounge Set, $41 with coupon (Originally $52)
  • Brooklinen Super-Plush Towels Set of Two, $79

Laneige Water Sleeping Face Mask

While I struggle with dry patches year-round, fall and winter are especially hard on my sensitive skin. That’s why hydrating products are essential in my routine, and the Laneige Water Sleeping Face Mask is next in my testing lineup. The product’s hero ingredient is squalane, which deeply moisturizes and protects the skin barrier, leaving you with a softer and silkier complexion. After cleansing your skin and applying face cream, the brand says to evenly apply the mask, let it absorb into your skin overnight, and rinse it off in the morning. As one shopper put it, you may just wake up feeling like your “skin drank a glass of water” — sign me up. 

Dazzle Dry Nail Mini Lacquer

I’ve been exclusively wearing Dazzle Dry nail polish for the past six months, and my nails are stronger than ever before. Of course, I have to get new fall colors, and this vampy purple shade is at the top of my list. The formula legitimately dries within five minutes, and it lasts more than seven days without chipping — I’m talking gel-level staying power. Plus, it comes in mini bottles, like the one above, so you can try out different colors before committing to a full-size container. 

Ugg Classic Ultra Mini Fashion Boot

It’ll come as no surprise that a pair of mini Uggs made its way onto my list, but it’s worth pointing out that many popular colorways, including chestnut and black, are well-stocked on Amazon right now. I’ve worn the same Ugg boots nonstop for the past few years now, so I’m finally getting myself a fresh new pair this fall. They’re the perfect shoes to slip on for casual hangs, running errands, and even going out to a low-key dinner. I know I’ll be wearing my pair pretty much every day for the next few months. 

Amazon Essentials Cable Knit Sweater

 I’ll take any excuse to get a new sweater, but a $24 price tag is a pretty good one. This Amazon Essentials cable knit sweater is made from a cotton blend that’s “very soft and comfortable” and “not itchy whatsoever,” per a reviewer. It comes in sizes XS through XXL and eight colors, though my personal favorite is the espresso brown, since it gives off cozy fall vibes. I’ll style the crewneck pullover casually with jeans and chunky Chelsea boots, or dress it up with a silky midi skirt and heeled booties. 

Paddywax Cypress and Fir Hand-Poured Scented Candle

A fall shopping haul wouldn’t be complete without a candle, and I’ve landed on this Paddywax ribbed glass option. The single-wick candle has a holiday-inspired scent (it’s never too early, okay?), with notes of fir needles, white eucalyptus, pine cones, and sandalwood for a warm and woodsy vibe. I can just picture myself lighting this candle while I lay on the couch under a cozy blanket watching a nostalgic movie. I feel relaxed just thinking about it.
