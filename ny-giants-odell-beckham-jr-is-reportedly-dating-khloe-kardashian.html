NY Giants' Odell Beckham Jr Is Reportedly Dating Khloe Kardashian

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Wednesday, August 14, 2024

If you're a New York Giants fan you might want to take a seat. Odell Beckham Jr. is reportedly dating Khloe Kardashian.

There was also some footage of the two hanging out this weekend at 1Oak in Las Vegas but nothing that suggested the two were an item.

Apparently, this isn't a new thing though, as the two have been seeing each other on the low for a few months and are ready to let the world know what it is.
