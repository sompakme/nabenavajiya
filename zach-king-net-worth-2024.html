Early Life and Career Beginnings

Zach King was born on February 4, 1990, in Portland, Oregon. He developed an interest in filmmaking at a young age and started making videos when he was just seven years old. He later attended Biola University, where he studied Cinema and Media Arts.

Rise to Fame

King’s rise to fame began when he started posting videos on Vine, a short-form video hosting service. His “magic vines”, which often involved him performing magic tricks, quickly gained popularity and earned him millions of followers.

Transition to YouTube and Instagram

After Vine shut down in 2016, King transitioned to other platforms like YouTube and Instagram. His unique content continued to attract millions of viewers, further increasing his popularity and wealth.

Brand Collaborations and Endorsements

Awards and Recognition

Real Estate Investments
