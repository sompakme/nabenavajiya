Remas O2 Arena Show: Videos of Traumatised Concertgoers Reactions to Bizarre Imagery Trend

Posted by Martina Birk on Tuesday, September 17, 2024
  • Rema's O2 Arena concert in the UK has continued to trend as imagery from the show stirred a heated debate online
  • A female fan who attended the concert revealed she was traumatised and had to leave the show early
  • A clip also showed another lady doing the cross sign as she watched Rema perform, and this stirred reactions online

Nigerian international star Divine Ikubor Rema's recent concert at the prestigious O2 Arena in the UK has stirred up controversies about the singer and his art.

Legit.ng recalls reporting that some Nigerians had linked Rema to the world secret society, the Illuminati, over some of the imageries used at his recent London show.

Traumatised fans who attended Rema's show speak

While some of Rema's fans who attended the O2 Arena concert had a great time, others left feeling disturbed.
