Kendall Jenner Proved That Khlo Is the Most Gullible Kardashian

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Wednesday, August 7, 2024

Kourtney Kardashian may be the "least-exciting" sister to look at and Kim is the most GIF-able sibling, but in a new video for Poosh, Kendall Jenner proved that Khloé is the most gullible. All it takes is a wedding ring and a quick snapshot. In the clip, which shows Kendall and Kourtney playing truth or dare together (and promoting Jenner's 818 tequila), the two sisters prank Khloé and she falls for it, just like Jenner predicted.

About halfway through the clip, Kourtney talks about how lenient and strict their parents are, which leads to Kourtney daring Kendall daring her to text the family group chat (also, can we get in on that?) and tell them that she got engaged.

"Mom's going to be like, 'What is happening?'" Jenner says before adding, "But it's believable, so I will do it."

Naturally, Jenner has to borrow a wedding ring from a crew member and Kourtney predicts that Khloé will "write a novel" about the news.

Instead, she gets calls immediately, and Kim accuses her sister of lying about the engagement. Khloé? She brushes it off, but her tone tells it all. She believed it.

Although Jenner was clearly joking, she's not the only Kardashian sibling that can stoke rumors about an engagement. Khloé recently showed off a huge iceberg-size diamond ring on Instagram, which had fans clamoring to know whether or not she was ready to get married again. The fact that she posted the snapshot on April Fools' Day helped clear things up — a little.

Back in July, only days after it was reported that she and Tristan Thompson reconciled, Khloé appeared to address engagement rumors, saying, "I just came online and I'm even confused lol."
