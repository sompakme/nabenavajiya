Kourtney Kardashians Matcha-filled aesthetic makes us green with envy Wed 23 February 2022 1

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Saturday, August 3, 2024

Kourtney Kardashian has brought a whole new meaning to ‘Matcha’, the green cup of coffee which basically goes with her whole aesthetic look. If you’re wondering what the whole thing means, we’ve got it covered.

When Kourt found happiness with Travis Barker, KUWTK fans everywhere were obsessed with their relationship. Much like Megan Fox and MGK’s vibe, they became couple goals almost instantly.

The same goes for Kourtney’s Matcha aesthetic. She even has her own recipe for the drink. From her own version of baked cookies with the ingredient, to tea and pudding, the reality star makes us green with jealousy.

KUWTK: ‘Matcha’ meaning

Matcha is essentially powdered green tea leaves, dissolved in hot water to make tea or used as a flavouring. It has become more popular over recent years, with the leaves traditionally ground by granite stones into a fine powder.

The Kardashians have been sipping on matcha lattes ever since the drink became popular. Kourt in particular is the most obsessed, and doesn’t go a day without having a taste of the green tea beverage.

As the new trailer of KUWTK recently hit HULU, many wonder what the term ‘matcha’ means. While it is just a drink, it is known for being the ‘green drink’ that Kourtney is often spotted drinking during episodes.

Kourt K’s Matcha aesthetic

If you’re a fan of Kourtney’s, you’ll already know just how much she loves matcha. She also winds down from every day with a cup of adaptogenic tea, including Buddha Teas Ashwagandha Tea. 

Her go-to Starbucks order is a matcha green tea. The warm caffeinated drink allows her to get energized without the jittery feeling that a black coffee used to bring her, before she waved goodbye to coffee when she was around 22.

Kourt also recommends Yogi Green Tea Super Antioxidant, an organic green tea that contains grapeseed and amla extracts, which aim to help fight free radicals in the body, according to She Knows.

While she genuinely loves the taste of matcha, it totally goes with her whole chilled vibe. Come on, who can work a trench coat and matcha latte for an added accessory better than Kourt?!

Her fave green recipe

Kourt can make a creamy green latte, and has even incorporated the caffeine flavours into her Collagen line, which is sold through her own brand Poosh. It’s even a staple drink at the company’s meetings…

The ingredients include: 1/2 teaspoon matcha, 2 cups of almond milk, and 1/4 cup organic maple syrup. It takes just five minutes of preparation for this Kourt-certified latte, and literally takes seconds to make.

Step-by-step of how to make Kourt’s matcha latte:

  • Boil milk.
  • Melt the matcha with a little boiling water.
  • Add the matcha and the syrup in the milk and beat very well.
  • If you want to be really boujee, serve in Kourt’s favorite double-wall mug (or your own!).
