The footballer's eldest daughter is on Love Island

    Michael Owen has made a candid confession in regard to his mental health. In a re-surfaced extra from his 2020 autobiography, , the former England footballer revealed he sought "professional help" after he grew "jealous" of his wife Louise's close bond with their eldest daughter, Love Island star Gemma Owen.
