Al Rokers wife Deborah Roberts astounds fans with paycheck reveal

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Monday, July 29, 2024

The ABC News star caused quite a reaction

    Al Roker’s wife Deborah Roberts has built herself a successful career - but she was just as surprised as her fans when she revealed the paycheck she received for appearing in the hit show .

    MORE: Al Roker's wife Deborah Roberts pays heartbreaking tribute to late brother

    The ABC News star took to Instagram last year to share a photo of the payment for her work – and what she earned left her followers gobsmacked. Deborah was even beside herself after showing off her check for the amount of three cents, telling her followers: "I won’t spend this all in one place!

    "I always marveled at actors who banked residuals after appearing in commercials and TV series. Well, I’m now in the club after an appearance in the hit show years ago," she added.

    "I’ve had a few small checks over the years …even as little as $7 …. but never one as memorable as this one! Had to share my #laugh with you!"
