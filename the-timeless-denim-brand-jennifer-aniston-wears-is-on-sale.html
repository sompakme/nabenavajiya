The Timeless Denim Brand Jennifer Aniston Wears Is on Sale

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Friday, August 2, 2024

Finding the perfect pair of jeans has been a lifelong process. I’ve spent countless hours searching through piles of vintage denim as well as countless paychecks splurging on brands I was sure would finally make me fall in love with jeans. And along the way, I’ve found some real (admittedly expensive) winners, but everytime I’m in need of a pair that will fit like a glove without breaking the bank, I turn to one iconic brand that’s a tried-and-true celebrity staple.

Levi’s has always been that brand, with classic jean styles that have won over everyone from Kylie Jenner to Jennifer Aniston. In fact, Aniston gave a shout out to the denim brand in a WSJ Magazine interview in August, saying that a pair of “Levi's and a T-shirt” is the look that will never go out of style. And I couldn’t agree more, so ahead of Amazon’s Prime Big Deal Days, I set out to find on-sale Levi’s jeans to add to my cart, and discovered that shopper-favorite styles are currently up to 76 percent off.

314 Shaping Straight Jeans

The 314 Shaping Straight Jeans are currently just $17 in the dark indigo wash in select sizes (but you can snag other washes up to 40 percent off, too). This style is stretchy and designed to enhance curves, with one shopper saying that they "fit perfectly...flattening your stomach [and] lifting the rear." According to another customer, these "fit like a glove" with "just the right stretch."

Classic Straight Jeans

Straight-leg jeans have a simple, goes-with-everything silhouette that’s timeless. That’s why I’m grabbing this Classic Straight pair from Levi’s that’s currently 49 percent off. The jeans boasts more than 7,000 five-star ratings at Amazon, where customers love the the “very soft, almost velvety” denim that has a “hint of stretch [that] helps adjust” to shopper’s curves. “[There’s] no gapping at the waist… [they’re] very flattering for my curves, [and they] make my butt look good,” raved a fan who noted that they had long struggled to find jeans that fit their curvy figure.

Low Pro Jeans

Kylie Jenner made Levi’s Low Pro Jeans part of her summer uniform, and now I’m eyeing them for fall. The 100-percent cotton style is loose (though not oversized) and features a mid- to low-rise that shoppers call “super flattering.” According to one shopper, these aren’t the low-rise you knew from the ‘90s and early 2000’s “in the best possible way.” “[They] hit just below the belly button, which is actually pretty flattering,” they explained, adding that even though they usually opt for high-rise, they’re loving this style that makes them “feel like a cool mom.” “They have a magical way of making your rear end look fantastic,” wrote another fan, adding that the Low Pro style is perfect for those wanting that “effortlessly chic, laid back vibe.”

94 Baggy Jeans

Of course, no bottoms style has been hotter in 2023 than baggy jeans, so you might as well hop on the trend while they’re on sale. The 94 Baggy Jeans are now 40 percent off and available in a faded black that’s perfect for fall. As someone who has larger hips and thighs, I’ve struggled to find baggy jeans that actually hang without looking frumpy, but according to “thicker” shoppers, these fit loosely “like a boyfriend style.”. Another customer was impressed by the style’s versatility, explaining that “they look super cute with a T-shirt and sneakers,” but that they also “wear them to work all the time too.”

Tons of Levi’s are on sale during Amazon’s Prime Big Deal Days. Stock up on more styles from the celebrity-favorite brand, below.
