Maria Thattil is a popular writer, YouTuber, Influencer, activist, makeup artist, model, speaker, content creator, and social media personality from Australia, who was born on 04 February 1993. She came into the spotlight after winning the title of ‘Miss Universe Australia’ in 2020. 

Moreover, in May 2021, she is representing Australia in the Global Miss Universe Competition. She has been called as a guest in many TV and Lives shows to know her success story. 

Maria Thattil is also a makeup artist and social activist. She hosts a podcast named ‘Mind with Me’ on social media, on which she discusses the prevailing social evils. Many people join her in this podcast to listen to her encouraging words and motivational speech. 

Biography

According to her official website, Maria took birth on February 04, 1993, in Melbourne, Australia. Her date of birth follows Aquarius’s horoscope. Maria Thattil visits various countries and cities for her shows and interviews. 

After completing basic education from her hometown school, she did major in Psychology. Further, she took admission to Melbourne Business School for her Master’s degree in Management. A few months ago, Maria has done a Neurocoaching qualification, which is an internationally accredited degree.

Family & Ethnicity

Maria Thattil’s parents are migrants of India. Her father was from Kerala and her mother was from Kolkata. In the early ’90s, her family shifted to Melbourne. Maria also visits India to meet her father’s side family. 

She often uploads pictures with her brother named Dominic Thattil. Her brother supports her to pursue in modeling competitions. She belongs to a mixed (Asian) ethnic group.
