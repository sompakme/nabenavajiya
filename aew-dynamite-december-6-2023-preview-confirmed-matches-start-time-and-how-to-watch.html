AEW Dynamite December 6, 2023 Preview: Confirmed Matches, Start Time and How To Watch

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Saturday, July 13, 2024

AEW has a lot of fans talking right now as they are carrying on toward December 30th’s Worlds End pay-per-view. On December 6, 2023, AEW Dynamite is scheduled to take place at the Target Center in Minneapolis, Minnesota. This week’s episode of AEW Dynamite already has several things booked, including matches that will directly affect the upcoming pay-per-view.

AEW Dynamite will air at 8:00 PM EST on TBS in the United States, and it will mark three weeks since their Full Gear pay-per-view.

This week’s episode promises to be an action-packed show, broadcasting live from the Bell Centre in Montreal. The highlight of the evening is a highly anticipated singles match between Adam Copeland and Christian Cage, marking their first one-on-one encounter in over 13 years. The stakes are high as they battle for Cage’s TNT Championship. Additionally, the show will serve as a stepping stone towards next month’s Worlds End PPV.

Another must-see match will feature the World Champion, MJF, teaming up with his Worlds End challenger, Samoa Joe, to take on a mysterious and unannounced team sent by The Devil himself. The AEW Women’s World Championship will be up for grabs as the ever-impressive Toni Storm defends her title against the formidable Skye Blue.

The excitement doesn’t stop there, as the Continental Classic tournament continues with thrilling matchups, including Jon Moxley facing off against RUSH, Swerve Strickland going head-to-head with Mark Briscoe, and a clash between Jay White and Jay Lethal.

And that’s not all! Be prepared for even more surprises and thrilling action as we gear up for a night of unforgettable moments at the Bell Centre in Montreal.

AEW Dynamite December 6th, 2023 Card:

  • TNT Championship Match: Christian Cage (c) vs. Adam Copeland
  • Special Introduction by Ben Mankiewicz for Toni Storm
  • AEW Women’s World Championship Match: Toni Storm (c) vs. Skye Blue
  • Tag Team Showdown: MJF & Samoa Joe vs. The Devil’s Goons
  • AEW Continental Classic Gold League Matchup: Jay White vs. Jay Lethal
  • AEW Continental Classic Gold League Clash: Jon Moxley vs. RUSH
  • AEW Continental Classic Gold League Bout: Swerve Strickland vs. Mark Briscoe
