How do you start a John Deere 100 Series lawn tractor?

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Tuesday, August 6, 2024

Answer

If the John Deere lawn tractor engine is still warm, just half of the throttle should be pulled off. Keep your foot on the brake pedal. To begin, move the throttle lever to the “Start” position on the dashboard. To start the lawn tractor, turn the key in the ignition.
