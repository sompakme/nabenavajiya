The Mysterious World of 30 Coins

“30 Coins,” created by Álex de la Iglesia for HBO Europe, centers around Father Vergara, an exorcist sent to the secluded Spanish village of Pedraza. The series, directed and written by Álex de la Iglesia, debuted on November 29, 2020, on HBO Europe. Season 2, which started filming in February 2022, hit screens on October 23, 2023, immersing viewers in a suspenseful journey filled with supernatural encounters.
