Free Ackrite Drops Off West Coast Banger &quot;Stand Down&quot;

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Wednesday, October 2, 2024

Ever since Hittman told ya'll to ackrite on the underrated 2001 banger, the word has been firmly entrenched in West Coast lexicon. For some, the slang has become more - a way of life. Such is the case for Los Angeles rapper Free Ackrite, who has been quietly holding it down with fire for years now. Today, the Cozz affiliate has come through with a new banger called "Stand Down." Taking to G-Funk production with effortless flow, Ackrite issues a warning to all would-be competition. "They say I'm the hood too much, but ain't no such thing," raps Ackrite, "we catchin' bodies or we at the port tussling." 

The NyneSix banger is a worthy find for anyone seeking to expand their West Coast repertoire. Be sure to show some support to Free Ackrite, who has been steadily rising for a minute. The future looks bright.
