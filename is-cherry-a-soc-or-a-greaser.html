What is cherry for the Greasers?

How is Cherry described in The Outsiders?

Cherry is not just a sweet, simple girl. She finds herself attracted to Dally, who is crass and unrefined but also sexy and charismatic. Despite all her attraction to the greasers, moreover, she is not completely free of group prejudice.

Is Ponyboy his real name?
