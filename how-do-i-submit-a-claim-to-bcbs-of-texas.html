Conquering the Claim Beast: How to Submit a Claim to BCBS of Texas (Without Pulling Your Hair Out)

Ah, yes, claims. The bane of every patient's existence and a provider's not-so-secret nemesis. But fear not, valiant healthcare warriors! This guide will be your trusty broadsword in the battle against claim confusion for Blue Cross and Blue Shield of Texas (BCBS of Texas).

Choosing Your Weapon: Electronic or Paper?

First things first, how are you wielding this claim? In the age of digital dominance, electronic submission is highly recommended (and probably the reason your fax machine collects dust bunnies). BCBS of Texas offers a free tool called Availity® that allows you to submit claims with lightning speed. Think of it as your digital Excalibur.

Paper claims are still an option, but they're like battling a dragon with a spork. It can be done, but why make life difficult?

Pro Tip: If you're a healthcare provider and haven't signed up for Availity® yet, it's like leaving your shield at home. Do yourself a favor and get registered!

The Paper Path (for the Truly Brave)

So, you've chosen the paper path. Here's what you'll need in your claim backpack:

  • The Standard CMS-1500 or UB-04 Claim Form: This is your battle map. Fill it out accurately and completely. Remember, even the mightiest knight stumbles with bad directions.
  • Patience: Paper claims take longer to process, so grab a good book (or fifteen).
  • A Filing Deadline Superpower: You have 95 days from the date of service to submit your claim. Don't miss this deadline, or your claim gets banished to the reimbursement graveyard.

The Electronic Highway (the Preferred Route)

Availity® is your shining chariot to claim submission bliss. Here's how to use it:

  • Log in: If you haven't already, register for free. No horse training required here.
  • Submit those claims!: It's user-friendly and lets you track the status of your claims. Like a knight checking his compass, you'll always know where your claim is headed.

Remember, Warrior:

  • Accuracy is Key: Double-check all your information before submitting. A tiny typo can turn your claim into a damsel in distress.
  • Keep Copies: Just like any good adventurer, keep a record of your submitted claims for future reference.

By following these steps, you'll be a claim-submitting champion in no time. Remember, conquering the claim beast is all about using the right tools and a healthy dose of patience. Now go forth and get reimbursed!
