Biography

Ryan Jacob Paevey-Vlieger (born 24 September 1984), addressed professionally as Ryan Paevey, is a multifaceted American model, actor, and TV personality.

He gained notoriety from 2013 to 2018 for his portrayal of Nathan West on the ABC serial opera General Hospital. His 2016 telefilm Unleashing Mr. Darcy and its 2018 follow-up, Marrying Mr. Darcy, starred him as Donovan Darcy, a highly well-known character. 

His other well-known telefilms include Dude Ranch, Harvest Love, Hope at Christmas, 4 Dead Girls: The Soul Taker, and From Friend to Fiancé.

His lone cinematic appearance to date was in 2016’s Bogie and Bacall. Ryan Paevey began his career as a model and has since produced music videos for songs by Toni Braxton, Robin Thicke, and others.

When he was younger, Ryan Paevey assisted his father with construction work and occasionally worked as a bartender, but he never intended to work in show business. However, he received numerous offers to work as a model, which he initially resisted on several occasions but ultimately accepted. 

He served as Robin Thicke‘s body double in 2009 for the singer’s song Sex Therapy music video. He appeared in the music video for Toni Braxton‘s single Hands Tied the following year.

Career

While Ryan Paevey‘s part in the TV show General Hospital has been his most notable professional accomplishment yet, he has also contributed to several made-for-television movies, among other things. He starred as Donovan Darcy, the male lead, in the Hallmark Channel original film Unleashing Mr. Darcy in 2016.

Due to the telefilm’s success, Marrying Mr. Darcy‘s sequel was released in 2018, and he returned to play the lead. In the interim, he acted as Will Nash in the 2017 electronic film Harvest Love and Mac in the 2018 holiday film Hope At Christmas. He appeared in the telefilms Dude Ranch and From Friend to Fiancé in 2019. 

In 2014 and 2015, Ryan Paevey also appeared as a guest co-host on Extra and The View. He served as the Outstanding Morning Program award presenter at the 42nd Daytime Emmy Awards in 2015. 

In the 2016 romantic comedy Bogie and Bacall, he played the part of Adlai Stevenson. In the 2019 romantic comedy A Summer Romance, he played Richard Belmont.

Hiking is Ryan Paevey‘s preferred pastime because he considers himself a nature boy. Lillith and Sage, his two motorcycles, are his favorites for travel. His followers always requested the intriguing photos he frequently captured while traveling.
