Yay Yay

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Wednesday, July 3, 2024

Check out Yay Yay (Prod. By Boi-1da), the latest track from ScHoolboy Q, which dropped on Friday, March 22nd, 2013. ScHoolboy Q's movement grows stronger with each new release, and Yay Yay (Prod. By Boi-1da) has further strengthened that fact. It's a solid addition to the commendable body of work ScHoolboy Q has been creating since stepping foot in the rap game, and we're definitely looking forward to what ScHoolboy Q has on deck.
