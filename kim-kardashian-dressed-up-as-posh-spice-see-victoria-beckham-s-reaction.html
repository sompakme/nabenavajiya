Kim Kardashian dressed up as Posh Spice see Victoria Beckham's reaction

Posted by Martina Birk on Monday, September 16, 2024

    Dressing up as a Spice Girl was something of a regular occurrence among teenage girls in the nineties, and Kim Kardashian has shown she was no different. The reality TV queen shared a throwback photo on Instagram of herself channelling the slickest of the Spice Girls â€“ Posh Spice.

    Wearing a black leather mini dress that featured a thigh-high split, Kim showed off her famous curves in the revealing number.

    She paired Victoria Beckham's former signature look with strappy heels, and had her raven tresses styled into the singer's straight bob.

    Victoria Beckham praised Kim Kardashian's efforts, calling the photo "genius"

    The throwback photo, which was taken during Kim's school days, received a lot of love and appreciation from Victoria, who reposted the image on her own Instagram account.

    It sounds like Victoria, who attended the British Fashion Awards with her husband David earlier this week, has a fan in Mrs Kardashian-West.

    Kim, who posted the images on Thanksgiving Day, explained that she "thankful" for popular nineties band the Spice Girls and in particular for Posh Spice.
