Maja Salvador Career

Maj Salvador started her career with a minor role in the drama series It Might Be You, It revolved around two childhood friends, who fall in love. The television show broadcast from 2003 to 2004.

In 2004, she played the main role in the television series Spirits. It revolved around eight children who discover they have outstanding abilities.

In 2006, Maja made her debut in the horror movie Sukob the film was an immediate success, and Salvador won multiple awards for her performance. The same year, she worked in another film First Day High.

Salvador appeared in numerous film tv shows during her great success like Pangarap na Bituin (2007), Komiks Kapitan Boom (2008), Nagsimula sa Puso (2009), and Imposter (2010).

In 2011, Salvador co-produced as well as played the main role in the film Thelma, she carried on her career in films such as Status: It’s Complicated (2013) and My Cactus Heart (2012). The Legal Wife (2014), Bridges of Love (2015) and Wildflower (2017).

As a Singer, Salvador has released two albums whose name is Believe (2014) and Maja in Love (2015).
