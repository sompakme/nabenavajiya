60-Year-Old Woman Falls in Love With 27-Year-Old Man, Ready to Have Children for Him

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Sunday, September 15, 2024
  • Yusta Lucas revealed she was more than willing and ready to start a family with her 27-year-old hubby, Shine
  • The Tanzanian woman vehemently denied any rumours of her handsome husband having a baby mama or child
  • Yusta said she had money to raise another child who was not hers but added she was prepared to give birth and have a family

An elderly woman is ready to have children with her new husband following their one-of-a-kind wedding.

Yusta Lucas said she was prepared to give her handsome hubby, Shine, babies as they look to build a family.

The 60-year-old said that was their plan from the start, to live together happily ever after with kids of their own.

Woman, 60, ready for family

During an interview with Global TV Online, the sexagenarian said everything was in place for the couple to start a family.
