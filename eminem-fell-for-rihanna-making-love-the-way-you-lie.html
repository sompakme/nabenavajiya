Eminem Fell For Rihanna Making &quot;Love The Way You Lie&quot;

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Saturday, September 14, 2024

Eminem seems to have fallen for Rihanna during the time they spent together making "Love The Way You Lie". Eminem, who apparently wants to date Rihanna, was recently put on blast by his grandmother of all people. Eminem's grandmother Betty Kresin actually thinks that her grandson and Rihanna make a great couple. She also revealed that the two superstars are constantly texting and talking on the phone to each other. However, Rihanna is currently dating Matt Kemp, who plays baseball for the Los Angeles Dodgers, and is keeping the relationship with Eminem strictly platonic.
