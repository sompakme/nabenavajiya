Shop Travis Kelce Favorite Cologne Louis Vuitton Meteore

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Wednesday, July 17, 2024

Fans of Taylor Swift and Travis Kelce still have a lot of questions. Like, when did they really start dating? And: When is the next time we’ll see them together? One thing I’ve been wondering as a beauty editor is: What does Travis Kelce smell like? Well, now we know: The professional football star’s signature scent is allegedly Louis Vuitton Météore—a clean, citrusy cologne with notes of mandarin orange, pink pepper, and vetiver.

Louis Vuitton Météore

Louis Vuitton Météore

Back in 2022, Fox Sports reporter Erin Andrews even called the Kansas City Chiefs tight end the “best-smelling” player in the NFL, on her podcast. She elaborated during a recent Today appearance with Hoda Kotb and Jenna Bush: “I sat down with him—he smells like a dream. He’ll say, ‘It’s Louis, baby.’” The trio went on to say Kelce is “one of those guys that gets in an elevator and smells good.”

Andrews said Kelce identified the cologne as Météore after she texted him to find out the name for her podcast. The scent was created by perfumer Jacques Cavallier Belletrud for Louis Vuitton and is meant to evoke “boundless energy,” as well as “power and light.” Fans of the fragrance liken it to fresh laundry. But perhaps the best compliment? On Today, Andrews noted that after hugging Kelce, she ended up smelling like him all day long—so the scent clearly sticks around. That longevity comes at cost: $450 for 6.8 fluid ounces, and $320 for 3.4 fluid ounces.

Kelce and Swift are both massive fans of Louis Vuitton, and in recent weeks, both have been spotted wearing accessories from the brand, like the singer’s go-to $3,450 Camera Box bag.

If you’re looking for a cologne that shares ozonic, fresh, and citrus notes similar to Météore’s, there are a few options. We shopped out classic scents below from Azzaro, Davidoff, and Calvin Klein—all under $109, making them perfect gifts ahead of the holiday season.
