Rihanna Barbados Crop Over Festival Outfit 2017

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Thursday, July 18, 2024

No one else should even bother getting dressed for the rest of 2017 because Robyn Rihanna Fenty just shut everything down with her look at the Crop Over Festival in Barbados.

Bad Gal Riri returned to her hometown of Barbados to attend the island's traditional harvest festival today—and she did not disappoint with her look. The singer showed up with blue hair wearing a jewel-encrusted bikini, pearlized d.bleu.dazzled tights, and matching thigh-high gladiators.

Topping off her look, Rihanna wore pink and green feathered angel wings, looking better than any Victoria's Secret Angel you've ever seen. And luckily for us, she shared a Boomerang on Instagram Stories showing off her incredible curves.

Body shamers who? Everyone can take a seat now.
