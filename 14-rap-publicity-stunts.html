14 Rap Publicity Stunts

Posted by Martina Birk on Tuesday, August 6, 2024

Smart, crazy, and sometimes just odd, publicity stunts can sometimes be a necessity for any rapper who wants to rise to the top, or just find themselves some quick fame. Whether it's to promote a tour, upcoming music, or just to get people talking, publicity stunts equate to media attention.

Of course, there is a fine art to orchestrating a stunt. Done correctly, they can help build careers. But, they can also break them. Whatever the repercussions, publicity stunts keep the rap game entertaining, and have led to some of the most unexpected, shocking, and sometimes hilarious moves in rap music.

Check out our list of mad publicity stunts featuring Eminem, Nas and Gucci Mane and let us know what you think are the most memorable stunts in hip hop.


50 Cent Threatens To Retire If Outsold By Kanye's

14 Rap Publicity Stunts

50 Cent Threatens To Retire If Outsold By Kanye West

On September 11th 2007, two huge hip hop albums, 50 Cent’s Curtis and Kanye West’s Graduation were released, going head-to-head. Making things a little interesting, 50 publicly announced he would retire if Curtis failed to outsell Yeezy’s Graduation.

Whilst Kanye remained fairly quiet amidst Fiddy’s fighting talk, Yeezy got the last laugh when Graduation outsold Curtis by 266,000 copies. But 50’s promise of retirement was nowhere to be seen.

He later revealed in XXL Magazine in 2012, that the whole thing was just good ol' marketing.

"That was something that we created. Obviously, it riled the fans up in every way and the results are always bigger sales than usual. Both of the numbers were bigger than what they would have been [otherwise]. We didn’t have no beef at all…. It was just great marketing."

50 Cent is definitely a damn good businessman.

Eminem & Sacha Baron Cohen At The 2009 MTV Movie Awards

Sitting with his entourage in the audience of the 2009 MTV Movie Awards, Eminem was outraged when Sacha Baron Cohen, dressed as alter ego Bruno, unexpectedly landed in the rapper’s lap from his descending wire. With both bare ass cheeks in Eminem’s face, Mathers completely lost it, shouting "Are you serious?! Are you fucking serious?!"

His entourage rushed in to push Cohen away from the rapper. Viewers and the audience were left stunned as Shady and his entourage then immediately exited the awards in disgust.

Three days later, Eminem admitted the gag was completely rehearsed with Cohen prior to the event.

“I’m thrilled that we pulled this off better than we rehearsed it. It had so many people going “nuts” so to speak. Everyone was blowing me up about it.”

Shady certainly showed off his impressive acting skills.

Gucci Mane's Twitter Rant

14 Rap Publicity Stunts

A few months ago, Gucci Mane’s three day Twitter rant shocked the hip hop world. It began when his rocky relationship with Waka Flocka Flame and Brick Squad came to an end when he tweeted, “Fuck Brick Squad”.

A tweeting tennis match proceeded between Brick Squad members and Gucci as he was accused of paying for rapper Young Vito’s attorney bills who stood accused of Slim Dunkin’s death. Gucci vehemently denied this.

On Day 3, Gucci decided to diss anyone and everyone from Drake, Frenchie, T.I., 2 Chainz, Tyga to name a few. He also claimed he had threesomes with Nicki Minaj, and Black Chyna both with Waka. 

Possible explanations surfaced, including that it was a huge marketing ploy, or even an ‘Amanda Bynes’ style meltdown. Gucci claimed his Twitter had been hacked by his ex-manager and apologetically released his forthcoming album Diary of A Trap God for free.

Following a hiatus, Mane returned to Twitter a few weeks later apologizing for the tweets. He also admitted he was in rehab due to a lean addiction.

DJ Khaled Proposes To Nicki Minaj

14 Rap Publicity Stunts

DJ Khaled recently left us bewildered when he proposed to fellow YMCMB star Nicki Minaj on MTV News. With a 10-karat radiant cut Rafaello & Co diamond ring worth $500,000, this initially seemed like no joke.

The stunt was kept up for the next few days. Pal Rick Ross even kept to the script saying Khaled was definitely “not playing”.

Yet the cat was let out of the bag when Hot 97 spoke to Nicki Minaj, “Khaled is my brother” and in reference to the proposal, “Nobody took it seriously.” It quickly became clear that this move was just to promote Khaled’s upcoming single, “I Want To Be” featuring Minaj.

Khaled later admitted that it was all one big fat publicity stunt.

“I was joking with it and having fun. We be so busy making these big hits and I wanted to come with a dope plan to present the next smash single besides just giving it out”.

Jay Z's "Retirement"

14 Rap Publicity Stunts

Considering Jay Z’s career highlights this year, this stunt is hard to believe. In 2003, Hov, aged just 34 announced that his forthcoming record, The Black Album would be his last, feeling he could no longer top himself.

Post retirement, Jay Z was not exactly missing from the public eye, making a collaborative album with Linkin Park, headlining New York’s Power 105.1 annual concert, not to mention him becoming President of Def Jam Records.

When Jay announced his comeback with the release of Kingdom Come in 2006, he was adamant that the whole thing was not a publicity stunt. Asked if he meant to retire he said, “I believed it, yeah, I believe it for two years”.

Embarrassed by it all, he later admitted that it was the “worst retirement in history”.

Havoc and Mobb Deep Twitter Beef

Mobb Deep had us baffled when Havoc began blasting fellow member Prodigy on Twitter in April 2012. Havoc sent a series of crazy tweets claiming Prodigy had gay sex while he was in prison.

“Prodigy a bitch and I'm gonna show it, follow!! ground breaking new. I got niggas in the jail system to back up that prodigy was fucking homes in jail. Prodigy prove me wrong! Please”.

The next day Havoc insisted he had nothing to do with these tweets. He claimed he had left his phone in a gas station. This made no sense considering the tweets were made through the internet. Nice try Havoc.

Havoc also dismissed Power 105.1 audio evidence of Havoc telling a friend that Prodigy was gay. 

Prodigy carried on with Havoc normally, much to everyone’s surprise.

Later, in an interview with Hot 97, Prodigy addressed the incident confirming that although those tweets weren’t made by Havoc, together they “milked it for what it’s worth”. 

2 Pac Claims He Slept With Faith Evans

In his track “Hit ‘Em Up”, 2 Pac famously claimed that he had slept with Faith Evans, Biggie’s wife.

“I ain’t got no motherfucking friends, that’s why I fucked your bitch”.

With the East Coast-West Coast rivalry at its height, Biggie simply took it as Pac’s way of getting to him. Evans, who worked on several tracks with 2 Pac, has always denied these allegations. But her confessions of being unfaithful during breaks in their tumultuous marriage have always created suspicion. 

It was definitely a raw assertion from Pac considering how crazy the beef between the East and West was.

It was a little unexpected when Papoose decided to turn up and perform on stage at this year’s Hot 97 Summer Jam. Hopping on after K Dot, the appearance looked strangely unplanned. It was also rumoured that French Montana’s set was cut short due to Papoose gate crashing the setlist.

Nas and Kelis Wear "NIGGER" Clothing

14 Rap Publicity Stunts

Nas and Kelis Wear 'NIGGER' Clothing To The Grammy Awards 2008

When Nas decided that he would name his forthcoming album Nigger, he definitely went in heavy on the marketing. The rapper and his then-wife Kelis turned up to the prestigious Grammy Awards that year rocking clothing labelled boldly with the word "NIGGER" to promote his upcoming record. Their outfits caused for some slightly awkward interviewing on the red carpet, as he provided his personal definition of the term as ‘ignorance’.

The choice of title eventually turned out to be too controversial and Nas ended up changing the album name to Untitled.
