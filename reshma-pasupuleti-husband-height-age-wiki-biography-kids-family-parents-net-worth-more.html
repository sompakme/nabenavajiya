Reshma Pasupuleti Career – Actress

Reshma Pasupuleti shared that she started working as an air hostess at Delta Airlines after completing her education. Afterward, she began working as a reporter or journalist for the TV5 channel. Her beautiful voice and way of interacting gave her an opportunity to enter the film industry.

As per the reports, she started working as Doctor Divya in a television serial titled ‘Love’ from 2009 to 2011. She has also worked in other serials such as Maragatha Veenai, Andal Azhagar, Uyire, Kannana Kanne, Abhi Tailor, Vilangu, and many more.

Moreover, Reshma also appeared in several films in Tamil, Telugu, and Malayalam languages like Velainu Vandhutta Vellaikaaran, Pei Mama, Masala Padam, Thiraikku Varadha Kathai, and others. In 2019, she got an opportunity to participate in the famous show titled ‘Bigg Boss Tamil 3’ 2019. Sadly, she was evicted from the show in the middle.
