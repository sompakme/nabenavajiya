How Much Is The Application Fee For Georgia Tech

Posted by Martina Birk on Wednesday, September 25, 2024

So You Wanna Be a Yellow Jacket? Cracking the Georgia Tech Application Fee Code

Ah, Georgia Tech. The land of ramblin' wrecks, stellar academics, and...well, a bit of a mystery surrounding application fees. Fear not, future engineer, scientist, or business whiz, for I am here to shed light on this most pressing financial question.

The Great Divide: Undergrads vs. Grads

First things first, Georgia Tech operates on a split fee system, just like they like their atoms: divided. Undergrads, listen up! Your application fee is a cool $75. International applicants, for reasons that likely involve currency conversion headaches, will pay a slightly steeper $85.

For our graduate student friends, the fee jumps a notch to $95. International grad applicants cough up an additional ten bucks, bringing their total to $105.

Facing the Fee-ancial Facts

Now, before you reach for your piggy bank (or your parents' credit card), here are some harsh realities to consider:

  • This fee is a non-refundable one-way trip. Apply wisely, grasshopper.
  • Don't be a scofflaw! Applications without the fee attached get tossed quicker than a freshman at a calculus exam.

Feeling Fee-ble? Fee Waivers to the Rescue!

Hold on, there's a light at the end of the tunnel! Georgia Tech offers application fee waivers for those who qualify. This could include academic superstars, diversity champions, or students who attend special recruitment events. Undergrads, check out the admissions office website for details. Grads, head over to the Graduate Education FAQ section for the scoop.

How to FAQs: Conquering the Georgia Tech Application Fee

  • How to avoid the application fee blues? Apply early and make sure you're a good fit for Georgia Tech!
  • How to snag an application fee waiver (undergrad)? Check the admissions office website for details on eligibility.
  • How to snag an application fee waiver (grad)? Head over to the Graduate Education FAQ section for the nitty-gritty.
  • How to pay the application fee? The online application portal accepts most major credit cards. So easy, a Ramblin' Wreck could do it (but hopefully they're focused on driving).
  • How to feel fantastic about conquering the application fee? High-five yourself! You're one step closer to becoming a Georgia Tech legend (or at least surviving the application process).

Now that you're armed with application fee knowledge, get out there and apply! Just remember, even with the fee, a Georgia Tech education is an investment that could pay off in a big way. Just try not to spend all your future earnings on those tasty Waffle House waffles.
