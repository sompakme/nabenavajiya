He Wasnt Good for Me: Olaide Oyedeji Opens Up On Failed Relationship, Ex-lover Reacts

Posted by Martina Birk on Thursday, August 8, 2024
  • Nollywood actress Olaide Oyedeji has revealed she was the one who left her first baby daddy, who is also an actor
  • Oyedeji noted that the thespian was a good man but not good enough for her, adding that her father wanted to disown her after that
  • The actress said at the time, she was in a relationship but still grappling with the same issues as singles

Actress Olaide Oyedeji has explained why she left her first baby daddy. She opened up about her relationship in a recent interview with her colleague Biola Bayo on her podcast Talk to B.

Legit.ng earlier reported that Oyedeji shared how she lost her first child because she was born preterm and also had an infection.

Speaking about her love affair, she stated that she was the one who left her baby daddy.

Oyedeji said her baby daddy was a good man, but noted he was not good enough for her.

She added that she was going through all the challenges that singles were going through despite being in a relationship at the time. 

The curvy actress explained that she felt the best thing was to quit and move on.

She, however, clarified that actor did not cheat on her.

Olaide Oyedeji says she would have cheated 

Speaking further about her affair with her ex-lover, the mother of four noted that she would have been the one to cheat on him.

The film star also revealed that when she informed her father about her decision to move on, he wanted to disown her.
