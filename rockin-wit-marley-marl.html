Rockin Wit Marley Marl

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Wednesday, October 2, 2024

Redman returns to HNHH with yet another freestyle from his upcoming Muddy Waters 2: The Preload tape. Going in over Dougie Fresh & The Get Fresh Crew's "The Show" (which originally featured a scene-stealing verse from Slick Rick), Red reminds listeners why he's a living legend, mixing humor and charisma in a way that few others have paralleled. 

Muddy Waters 2: The Preload is available for pre-order now over at Redman's official website via an exclusive VIP package. Order your copy today and you'll receive 1 hard copy CD, 1 limited edition MW2 t-shirt, 1 limited edition MW2 beanie, 1 Redman sticker pack, 1 Medtainer grinder, 1 pack of Redman rolling papers, 3 Noble cigars, 1 personalized autographed poster, and exclusive access to in-store sessions, concerts and more. Basically, everything but Red's house.

Quotable Lyrics:
