How Much Did Lsu Pay Georgia State

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Monday, July 29, 2024
People are currently reading this guide.

The Great GSU Payday, or How Much Does it Cost to Get Trampled by Tigers?

Ah, college football. A time for pageantry, passion, and...paying your respects to the almighty Tigers of LSU. But what if you're not exactly Alabama or Clemson? Well, then you might just be looking at a hefty paycheck for a not-so-gentle reminder of why they call LSU's stadium "Death Valley."

So, How Much Did LSU Shower Georgia State With in 2023?

Hold onto your helmets, folks, because we're talking a cool $1.6 million. That's right, LSU wasn't just paying for a game, they were paying for a chance to see if their roar was still enough to scare up a win (spoiler alert: it was).

But Why So Much Dough?

There are a couple of reasons why a bigger program like LSU coughs up a small fortune to play a team like Georgia State.

  • Strength of Schedule: Every team wants to look good come playoff time, and that means having a decent strength of schedule. Scheduling a winnable non-conference game helps pad that resume.
  • Filling Those Seats (and Concessions Stands): Let's be honest, LSU fans love their Tigers. And what better way to get them excited than a guaranteed win (well, almost guaranteed) on a Saturday afternoon? Those hot dogs and overpriced beers don't sell themselves, you know.
  • A Chance for Up-and-Comers: Okay, okay, this one might be a bit of a stretch for Georgia State in this particular case. But sometimes these guarantee games can be a launching pad for smaller schools. Hey, maybe next year they'll only lose by two touchdowns!

But Don't Cry for Georgia State, They're Laughing All the Way to the Bank

Let's face it, $1.6 million is a pretty good chunk of change, especially for a program that isn't exactly rolling in dough. That money can go a long way in terms of new equipment, facilities upgrades, or maybe even a new mascot costume that doesn't look like it escaped a rejected Muppet design meeting.

FAQ: How to Get a Piece of That Sweet Guarantee Game Action (Probably Not Gonna Happen, But We Can Dream)

  • How to Get Your Tiny School on the Schedule of a Powerhouse Program? Start by winning your conference...consistently. Then, maybe after a decade or two of dominance, you can send a very nicely worded email.
  • How to Negotiate a Higher Payday? Become the hottest upset pick in the nation. That'll get LSU's attention real quick.
  • How to Spend Your Guarantee Game Millions Responsibly? Invest in some good financial advisors. Seriously, that kind of money needs a plan.
  • How to Avoid Getting Destroyed by a Team with a Bigger Budget for Cleats Than You Have for Your Entire Program? This one's a tough one. Maybe focus on the moral victory?
  • How to Get Over the Heartbreak of Losing to a Team Whose Mascot is a Tiger? Retail therapy. Always retail therapy.
