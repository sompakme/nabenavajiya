What exactly is Nm torque?

The newton metre (also known as the newton-metre, symbol Nm, N m) is a unit of torque (also known as moment) in the International System of Units (SI). For the purposes of this definition, a newton metre is the torque produced by a force of one newton applied perpendicularly to the end of a moment arm that is one metre long.
