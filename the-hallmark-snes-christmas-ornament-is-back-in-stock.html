The Hallmark SNES Christmas Ornament Is Back in Stock

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Monday, September 23, 2024

Heads up, gamers of a certain age who are planning to decorate a Christmas tree this holiday season. The Hallmark SNES Christmas ornament is available to order from Amazon right now. It's been going in and out of stock (mostly out) since it went up for sale earlier this year. Grab it for the list price of $19.99 if you want it.

Hallmark Signature SNES Christmas Ornament

The ornament is slightly backordered at the time of this writing, but it's currently shipping out around November 2. That means it'll arrive in plenty of time before Christmas. But the date has been creeping back every hour or so, which would seem to indicate it may be gone before long. It's currently out of stock at Hallmark, for instance.

It's a fairly adorable little Super Nintendo console, complete with a tiny copy of Super Mario World loaded in it. Press the power button, and the little red light next to the controller input glows, and it plays music and sounds from the game. It's really cool. There's a video of it on the Amazon page. It's like last year's NES ornament, which also sold out quickly.

So if you want to hang this delightful mini-console on your tree this year, you'll want to mash that buy button before it's gone.
