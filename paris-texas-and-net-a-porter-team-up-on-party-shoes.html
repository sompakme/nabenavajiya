Paris Texas and Net-a-Porter team up on party shoes

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Saturday, August 10, 2024

Luxury Italian footwear label Paris Texas – known for its celebrity-approved high-octane heels and boots – has created a very festive collection of party shoes, in collaboration with Net-a-Porter for the Christmas season. The capsule features three styles in numerous colourways, all of which would make a very glamorous addition to your partywear wardrobe.

"We are so pleased to be partnering with Net-a-Porter to bring the ultimate party capsule to life," Annamaria Brivio, founder and creative director at Paris Texas told us. "The collection has been designed for the festive season – adding a touch of subtle glam to any outfit. Ideal for celebrations and beyond, it effortlessly elevates the spirit of joy and style."

The party capsule includes the Livia Mary-Jane pump, the Liz lace-up sandal, and the blunt-toe Stiletto sandal (which will be available soon), coming in variations of black, midnight blue, lipstick red, silver, and gold. With super glamorous touches, these designs are made to elevate your eveningwear wardrobe, but they also make for very versatile pieces that you will be able to team with pretty much anything.

Paris Texas

A brand worn and loved by the likes of Emily Ratajkowski, Kendall Jenner, Hailey Bieber and Nicola Peltz Beckham, Paris Texas is a great name to know for those extra special dressing moments. Brivio describes the aesthetic as a marriage between "Parisian elegance" and "Texan toughness", something you can certainly see in this particular collection.
