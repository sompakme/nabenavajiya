The Weeknd Is Launching His Own Sneaker Line With Puma

Posted by Martina Birk on Saturday, August 10, 2024

BAZAAR's September cover star, The Weeknd (Abel Tesfaye), is continuing his fashion takeover with a new collection. The singer collaborated with Puma on a new line of apparel and sneakers, set to hit stores this fall. Debuting first from the collection will be the “Parallel” hightop sneaker, which Tesfaye unveiled in a new series of photos and Instagrams.

Courtesy Derek Wood for Footwear News

The singer poses in an all-white pair of “Parallel" sneakers.

The first sneakers will retail for $220 and come in all-white and olive green color schemes. For the singer, it was important to make a shoe that was not only accessible to the masses, but that also included his sense of style. "I was involved in almost every aspect, but I was most focused on the silhouette,” he explained to Footwear News. “With XO and Puma, we really collaborated and worked on making the best product possible."

The shoes will come first, but "his street-style-focused T-shirts, denim jackets and pants will launch shortly after," Footwear News revealed.
