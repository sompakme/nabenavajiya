Alfie Allen’s Early Life

Alfie Allen was born in Hammersmith, London, to film producer Alison Owen and actor Keith Allen. His sister, Lily Allen, is a famous singer and songwriter. Allen’s interest in acting was sparked at a young age, and he attended the Fine Arts College, Hampstead, where he honed his acting skills.

Acting Career

Allen’s acting career began with small roles in films and television series. His breakthrough came when he was cast as Theon Greyjoy in “Game of Thrones.” His performance in the series earned him a Primetime Emmy Award nomination for Outstanding Supporting Actor in a Drama Series in 2019.

Other Notable Roles

Beyond “Game of Thrones,” Allen has appeared in several other films and television series, including “John Wick,” “The Predator,” and “Jojo Rabbit.” These roles have significantly contributed to his net worth.
